Hoping the year 2021 is better than 2020. It surely has to be with how last year went. With the horrible leadership in this country and the pandemic sweeping across the world, 2020 was one of the worst I can remember for the US.

2021 brings us hope. The vaccine for coronavirus came out in late 2020 and has been given to 2,000,000 so far in the US. We will have a new president and new leadership. Hopefully things will turn around and go back to somewhat normal.

I admit, I have enjoyed spending time at home with the kids and family but it does wear one down mentally. My daughter loves going to school and loves interacting with people. She needs to get back there to feel better. She also does gymnastics and has been out for about 9 months now. My boys don't seem to mind being home but they hate virtual learning. They would be better off at school.

We have not been able to do much out of the house. We don't go to the park, zoo, arboretum, or anything really.

I hope that changes this year. And I hope those fun activities still exist after this is all over.
