I am finally getting around to scanning paper documents. We seem to have kept nearly every piece of paper that has entered our home.

I am scanning documents and loading them into a software called [paperless-ngx](https://docs.paperless-ngx.com/). ~~paperless-ng~~ The software is running as a docker container on my Synology NAS. The documents will go through <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optical_character_recognition" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">OCR</a> so they can be easily searched later.

Making my way through two filing cabinets filled with paper is a daunting task. So far I’ve only managed to clear off surfaces that had paper to be filed. I haven’t even opened the filling cabinets yet.

I hope to get through as much as I can this summer.
